Multiplayer Programmer

Company Description

To create revolutionary, story-driven RPGs which go straight to the hearts of gamers — this is our mission. As a driven team of talented professionals from all around the globe, we want to earn our place as one of the world’s top video-game developers, push narrative boundaries, and build unforgettable stories that set and redefine the standards for the genre.



Job Description

The position involves close collaboration with development teams in Poland. You will be responsible for coordinating multiplayer requirements and designing and implementing cutting-edge networking solutions.


Qualifications

  • Excellent knowledge of C++ coding principles and practices with at least 3 years of professional experience

  • At least 3 years of relevant experience in the games industry

  • Good understanding of the problem of lag, prediction and correction

  • Understanding of network protocols such as HTTP, TCP & UDP

  • Knowledge of client/server architectures and the full network stack

  • Ability to work effectively in a team and independently when required
