Company Description

To create revolutionary, story-driven RPGs which go straight to the hearts of gamers — this is our mission. As a driven team of talented professionals from all around the globe, we want to earn our place as one of the world’s top video-game developers, push narrative boundaries, and build unforgettable stories that set and redefine the standards for the genre.



Job Description

The ideal candidate is a highly-organised, detail-oriented and self-directing individual who is passionate about games, is comfortable working with a variety of departments, and has experience with QA testing.This is a full-time, salaried position with an in-office, hybrid in-office or WFH model, based out of our office in Vancouver Canada. The candidate must be local to the studio location.





Qualifications

  • 1+ years experience in QA Testing for game development

  • Knowledge of various testing methodologies

  • Experience working in technical pipelines

  • Experience with console and next-gen testing

  • Experience working with multi-site test teams

  • Experience with Unreal Engine









Additional Information

  • Competitive salary with project-based bonuses

  • Responsible work-life balance with flexible hours and paid overtime

  • 26 days of paid vacation per year
